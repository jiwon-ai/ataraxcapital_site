The Firm

Systematic thinking for complex markets.

Atarax Capital studies the relationships between growth, inflation, liquidity, policy, and price behavior across global markets.

Our work is grounded in repeatable research, portfolio discipline, and a sober respect for uncertainty. We combine quantitative signals with macro context to identify opportunities while keeping risk management at the center of the investment process.
